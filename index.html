Alison's Mancala Game

    Objective: Collect more pieces than your opponent.

  1. Begin by selecting any pocket on your side. Gray goes first.
  2. Moving counter-clockwise, deposit one piece in each subsequent pocket until the pieces run out.
  3. If you run into your own Mancala (larger pockets on either side), drop one piece in it.
  4. If you run into your opponent's Mancala, skip it.
  5. If the last piece you drop is in an empty pocket on your side, capture that piece and any pieces in the pocket directly opposite.
  6. The game ends when all six pockets on one side of the Mancala board are empty.
  7. Practice your victory dance and count the number of pieces in each Mancala to determine who won.
